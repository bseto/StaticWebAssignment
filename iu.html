IU

Lee Ji Eun

History

Lee Ji Eun, born May 16, 1993, also known as her stage name IU, is a South Korean singer and actress.

She signed with LOEN Entertainment in 2007 when she was 14 as a trainee and began her music career at 15.

With the success of her 2011 albums, IU established herself as a formidable force on the music charts in Korea, and cemented her image as Korea's "little sister".
